Our Students

Cultivating Lifelong Learners

Learning Beyond the Classroom

At TEHS, we believe in nurturing students who are not just academically excellent but also lifelong learners. Our students engage in a variety of activities that develop critical thinking, collaboration, and personal growth.

Research & Innovation

Students conduct interdisciplinary research projects, including air quality studies and low-cost environmental solutions, developing strong analytical and experimental skills.

Communication Skills

Through debates, business pitches, and multilingual performances, students enhance their communication abilities while developing essential soft skills and teamwork.

Collaborative Learning

Our curriculum emphasizes group work and peer learning, with students regularly engaging in collaborative projects and leadership opportunities.

Student Development

Academic Integrity

Students and staff follow IB guidelines with proper citation and referencing practices. We've implemented an Ethical AI use policy to ensure responsible technology use in learning.

Service Learning

Students engage in community service projects, including library volunteering, youth tutoring, and arts/crafts fundraisers, making positive impacts in their community.

Independent Learning

Students take ownership of their education through online courses in Python, Mindfulness, and AI Prompt Engineering, developing self-directed learning skills.

Cultural Exchange

Through international trips, language courses, and art projects, students explore and celebrate diverse cultures while developing their unique identities.
